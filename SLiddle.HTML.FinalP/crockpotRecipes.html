CrockPot Recipes


Beef and Cheese Dip


ingredient measurement substitutions
Ground Beef 1 lb
Taco Seasoning 2 tbs
Velveeta Cheese 8 oz
Shredded Cheddar Cheese 2 cups
Milk 1/2 cup Whole Milk
Cream Cheese 1 Block Sour Cream
Diced Tomatoes 1 cup 1 can Rotel

In a large crockpot, add the cheeses (cream cheese, shredded cheddar cheese, Velveeta cheese, or any desired array of cheese) and begin melting, stirring occasionally. Once the cheese is melted, add milk and taco seasoning to taste.
Begin by browning the ground beef in a large pan on a stovetop. Once the beef is browned, lightly toss the beef in a strainer over a bowl to remove excess oil. Do NOT drain the oil into a drain, it is best practice to let the oil solidify to reuse in other recipes or throw it away.
Once the beef is strained, pour it into the crockpot and fold in. The dip is ready to serve and is best for 3-4 hours. This dip can be refrigerated and good for 2-3 days.


Buffalo Chicken Dip


ingredient measurement substitutions
Shredded Cooked Chicken 2 cups
Cream Cheese Block
Franks Hot Sauce 1/2 cup
Ranch Dip 1/2 cup

In a crockpot, add all of the ingredients and melt on low, stirring occasionally.
