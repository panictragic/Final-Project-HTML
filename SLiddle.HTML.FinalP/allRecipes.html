Cheesy Chicken and Broccoli Casserole

ingredient measurement substitutions
Chunk Chicken 2 cans whole rotisserie chicken
Cooked Rice 4 Cups Instant Rice
Cream of Chicken Soup 1 Can
Cream of Mushroom Soup 1 Can
Mayo 1 1/2 Cups Cream Cheese
Shredded Cheese 2 Cups
Garlic Powder 1/2 tsp
Onion Powder 1/2 tsp
Broccoli 2 Cups Any Frozen Veggies
Ritz Crackers 1 Cup Any Crackers

This recipe is great for quick meals with leftovers in the fridge. All canned ingredients should be strained before use.
In a large casserole dish, add all ingredients EXCEPT the crushed Ritz crackers and half a cup of shredded cheese. Mix until thoroughly incorporated, then top with the crushed crackers and cheese. Preheat oven to 350 f and cook until the edges are brown, about 25 minutes depending on the oven. Let cool to room temperature before refrigeration.


Mexican Casserole

ingredient measurement substitutions
Ground Beef 1lb
Beans 1 can Refried Beans
Tortilla Chips 1 bag Taco Shells
Diced Tomatoes 1 can 2-3 Whole Tomatoes
1 Small Onion
Shredded Cheese 2 Cups Cheddar, Colby Jack
Taco Seasoning 1 Pack
Sour Cream To Taste

Begin by browning the ground beef in a large pan on a stovetop. Once the beef is browned, lightly toss the beef in a strainer over a bowl to remove excess oil. Do NOT drain the oil into a drain, it is best practice to let the oil solidify to reuse in other recipes or throw it away. In a large casserole dish, start by creating a layer of tortilla chips, then layer the other ingredients over. The layers should go in order of tortilla chips, beans, ground beef, onions and tomatoes, and cheese. These layers can be done in whatever order desired, though. Preheat oven to 350 F and cook until cheese is melted. Let cool to room temperature before refrigerating.
