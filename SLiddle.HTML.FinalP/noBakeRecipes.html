No Bake Recipes

Dips


Clam Dip

ingredient measurement substitutions
Cream Cheese Block
Minced Clams 1 can

In a large mixing bowl, add a 1:1 ratio of cans of clam to blocks of cream cheese. If a hand blender is accessible, mix thoroughly. Otherwise, mix together the ingredients with a large fork. Keep Refrigerated.


Taco Dip

ingredient measurement substitutions
Refried Beans 1 can
Cream Cheese Block
Sour Cream 16 oz
Salsa 1 jar
Chopped Peppers, Onions, Lettuce

Mix together the cream cheese and sour cream with any additional seasoning. On a large plate or serving dish, begin creating layers of beans, cream cheese mix, and salsa. Once finished, top with shredded cheese, chopped peppers, onions, and/or lettuce.


Desserts


Snicker Salad

ingredient measurement substitutions
Green Apples 4
Snickers Bars 4-5
Cool Whip 8 0z
Vanilla Instant Pudding 1 small box
Milk 1/2 Cup
Caramel

Chop apples and Snickers bars. In a large mixing bowl, combine cool whip, the box of instant pudding until smooth. Add milk as necessary. Once at a pudding consistency, fold in the chopped snickers bars and apples. Top with caramel and keep refrigerated.
